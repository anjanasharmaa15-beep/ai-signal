# AI Signal
### The role-aware AI knowledge library

> Everything happening in AI — news, tutorials, research papers, product launches, and GitHub repos — automatically classified and filtered for your job role.

**Live demo →** [peaceful-bienenstitch-027699.netlify.app](https://peaceful-bienenstitch-027699.netlify.app)

---

## The Problem

The AI landscape moves faster than any individual can track. New models, agent frameworks, open-source repos, research papers, and product launches surface daily — scattered across blogs, GitHub, arXiv, newsletters, and social platforms.

Existing solutions are partial: newsletters show you today's news, search engines show you what you already know to look for. Nobody gives an AI professional a single, always-current, role-filtered view of everything that matters in their domain.

---

## What AI Signal Does

AI Signal is a living knowledge library for the AI world. You pick your role — Product Manager, Engineer, Researcher, Business Leader — and the feed changes completely. Every item is tagged by content type and domain, with a one-line explanation of why it matters for your specific role.

**Browse mode** — role-filtered library organised by domain category

**Search mode** — AI-powered semantic search across the library (live, powered by Claude API)
